Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

AttendX ("we", "us", "our") provides attendance management software to universities and other institutions ("Schools"). This policy explains what information we collect from Students, Lecturers, and Administrators (together, "Users") when a School uses AttendX, and how that information is handled.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on your role, AttendX may collect:

  • Account information: name, email address, matric/staff number, department, and school affiliation.
  • Location data: GPS coordinates captured at the moment a lecturer starts an attendance session, and at the moment a student checks in, used to confirm the student is within an approved distance of the session.
  • Face verification photo: a still photo captured from your device camera at check-in, used only to confirm a face was present at the point of check-in. This is a presence check, not identity/biometric matching — AttendX does not use this photo to identify who you are.
  • Attendance records: which sessions you created, attended, or were marked present for, and when.

2. How We Use This Information

  • To operate core attendance features — starting sessions, checking students in, and generating reports.
  • To verify a check-in is happening at the approved location and that a face is present at the time of check-in.
  • To let Lecturers and School Admins view and export attendance records for their own school.
  • To maintain the security of accounts and detect misuse of the platform.

3. Who Can See Your Information

Access is role-based. Students can see their own attendance history. Lecturers can see attendance for sessions they created. School Admins can see records for their own school only. The Super Admin manages the platform and school-level accounts but does not routinely access individual attendance photos or GPS data. We do not sell User data to third parties, and we do not share it with advertisers.

4. Data Retention

Attendance records, including GPS coordinates and check-in photos, are retained for as long as your account and your School's use of AttendX remain active, so that accurate attendance history is available for the academic term. You or your School Admin may request deletion of your account and associated records, subject to any academic record-keeping requirements your institution may have.

5. Data Security

AttendX uses Firebase (Google Cloud) for authentication and data storage, with role-based access rules that restrict each account to the data relevant to its role. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable technical measures to protect your data.

6. GPS Accuracy Notice

GPS verification is performed using your device's location services and is checked on the device at the time of check-in. Like any GPS-based system, accuracy can vary by device and environment.

7. Your Choices

  • You can decline camera or location permission, but doing so will prevent you from checking in to sessions, since both are required for verification.
  • You can request a copy of your data or ask your School Admin to have your account and records removed.

8. Children's Privacy

AttendX is intended for use by university and higher-institution students, lecturers, and staff. It is not directed at children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as AttendX's features evolve. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data can be sent through the Contact form on the AttendX homepage.
