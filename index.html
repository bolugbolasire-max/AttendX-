About AttendX

AttendX is a modern university attendance management platform designed to replace manual attendance sheets with intelligent digital verification. By combining QR Codes, GPS verification, Facial Recognition and role-based access control, AttendX provides institutions with a secure, accurate and efficient attendance solution.

How AttendX Works

1️⃣ Login

Students and lecturers securely sign into their respective portals.

2️⃣ Attendance Session

The lecturer creates a class attendance session and generates a unique QR Code.

3️⃣ Student Verification

Students verify their GPS location and facial identity before scanning the QR Code.

4️⃣ Attendance Recorded

Attendance is stored instantly and becomes available to lecturers, school administrators and the super administrator.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can students mark attendance outside campus?

No. GPS verification ensures students are within the lecturer's approved location.

Can one student mark attendance for another?

No. Facial recognition prevents impersonation.

Does AttendX work on mobile devices?

Yes. AttendX is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets and computers.

Can schools generate attendance reports?

Yes. Lecturers, School Admins and Super Admins can generate reports based on their permissions.
